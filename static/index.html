PassCake

Encrypted Cloud Password Manager

Share passwords with your colleagues quickly & securely!

How it works

We generate a secret key and use AES-256-CTR encryption to encode your password. The key, as well as your raw credentials, never leaves your browser. We store only your encrypted password which can only be unlocked when you provide a key.
